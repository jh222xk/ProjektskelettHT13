audio

With the audio tag you can embed audio files without a third-party plugin.

Example

<audio controls="controls">
    <source src="foo.ogg" />
    Your browser does not support the audio element.
</audio>

Browser support

  • Firefox 3.5
  • Chrome 4.0
  • Internet Explorer 9.0
  • Opera 10.5 (Partially 9.5-10.1)
  • Safari 4.0

video

With the video tag you can embed video files without a third-party plugin.

Example

<video src="foo.ogg" controls="controls">
    Your browser does not support the video element.
</video>

Browser support

  • Firefox 3.5
  • Chrome 4.0
  • Internet Explorer 9.0
  • Opera 10.5
  • Safari 4.0
